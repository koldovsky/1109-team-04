What services do your provide?

We offer all services related to pet care. These include grooming, training, dog walking, pet sitting, pet hotel, etc.

Do you have a professional vet?

Yes, we have 3 vets that specialize in different pets, from cats to parrots and exotic animals. Still, we're not a vet clinic, so our vets provide only consultations and do not perform any procedures.

What does a pet sitter do?

A pet sitter is a person that looks after your pets when you're out. It includes feeding, washing, providing walks and exercises, etc.

Can I leave my pet at your place?

Yes, you travel with peace of mind. We have a mini-hotel for all kinds of pets. It is spacious, comfortable, and fully equipped.

Can I order one-time pet sitting services?

Sure, if you need a pet sitter just once, you can order such a service. Also, you can request a pet sitter for 1-2 hours and decide whether you want to use our services later.
